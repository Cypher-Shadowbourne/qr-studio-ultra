QR Studio Ultra is a premium, offline-first QR code generator designed for speed, beauty, and absolute privacy. Unlike generic web-wrapped apps, it utilizes a custom-built Rust rendering engine to ensure every pixel is mathematically perfect.

✨ FEATURES:
• High-Performance Rendering: Powered by a compiled ARM64 Rust backend for lightning-fast, memory-safe generation.
• Custom Aesthetics: Support for gradients, custom "eye" shapes (Finder Patterns), and rounded data modules.
• Smart Logo Integration: Includes an interactive, frontend-optimized cropper to embed logos perfectly into your codes.
• Privacy by Design: 100% offline. No trackers, no analytics, no external calls. Your data never leaves your device.
• Professional Export: Save high-resolution PNGs directly to your gallery using native Android MediaStore APIs.

🇮🇪 SPECIALIZED TOOLS:
Includes a dedicated Irish Dog Tag validator. Ensure your pet's 15-digit microchip code is accurate and compliant with local requirements using our specialized UI flow.
